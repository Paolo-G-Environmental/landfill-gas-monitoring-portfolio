# Field Interpretation Summary

This sample dataset demonstrates how methane, carbon dioxide, oxygen, and static pressure can be reviewed to evaluate landfill gas well performance.

Higher methane with low oxygen generally indicates stronger landfill gas collection conditions. Elevated oxygen may suggest air intrusion, damaged well components, or excessive vacuum. Static pressure trends should be reviewed alongside gas composition before making wellfield adjustments.
