# Field Decision Log

## Overview
This log demonstrates how landfill gas monitoring data can be translated into real-world field actions. Each entry represents a typical well condition and the corresponding operational response.

---

## Well GW-01 — Normal Operation
**Conditions:**
- CH₄: > 50%  
- O₂: < 3%  
- Flow: Stable  
- Vacuum: Strong  

**Assessment:**
Well is operating within expected parameters.

**Action:**
- No adjustment required  
- Continue routine monitoring  

---

## Well GW-02 — Early Warning (Possible Condensate Issue)
**Conditions:**
- CH₄: Moderate  
- O₂: 3–4%  
- Flow: Slightly reduced  
- Condensate: Possible  

**Assessment:**
Potential early-stage condensate buildup affecting flow.

**Action:**
- Inspect condensate sump and piping  
- Verify pump operation  
- Monitor for further flow reduction  

---

## Well GW-03 — Air Intrusion & Low Gas Quality
**Conditions:**
- CH₄: < 40%  
- O₂: > 5%  
- Flow: Low  
- Vacuum: Weak  

**Assessment:**
High likelihood of air intrusion and reduced gas quality.

**Action:**
- Reduce vacuum incrementally to limit air intrusion  
- Inspect wellhead seals and connections  
- Check for cracks or leaks in piping  
- Re-evaluate after adjustment  

---

## General Decision Framework

### Air Intrusion (O₂ > 5%)
- Reduce vacuum  
- Inspect well integrity  
- Check for leaks or damaged seals  

### Low Methane (CH₄ < 40%)
- Evaluate extraction rate  
- Check for over-pulling  
- Assess waste decomposition stage  

### Low Flow (< 20 SCFM)
- Inspect for blockages  
- Check valve position  
- Investigate condensate accumulation  

### Weak Vacuum (> -3 in. WC)
- Evaluate blower performance  
- Inspect system balance  
- Check for line restrictions  

### Condensate Issues
- Inspect sumps and pumps  
- Verify drainage  
- Remove liquid obstructions
